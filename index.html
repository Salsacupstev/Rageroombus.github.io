Welcome to Monkey Rodeo!

Are you ready for the most exciting and hilarious show on earth? Come and see Whiplash the Cowboy Monkey[^1^][2] and his trusty border collie ride at high speeds and chase after sheep! Whiplash is a white-faced capuchin monkey who has been riding since age two and has appeared on TV shows, commercials, and rodeos across the country. He is also a knighted cowboy monkey who loves to wear his cowboy hat and boots. Don't miss this chance to see him in action!

Monkey rodeo is not only fun, but also educational. You will learn about the amazing abilities of monkeys and dogs, as well as the history and culture of rodeo. You will also get to interact with Whiplash and his friends after the show and take pictures with them. Monkey rodeo is suitable for all ages and occasions, whether you are looking for a family outing, a birthday party, or a corporate event. Monkey rodeo will make you laugh, cheer, and clap like never before!
