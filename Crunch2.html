Our crunchwraps are made with fresh ingredients, homemade sauces, and crispy tortillas. You can choose from a variety of flavors, such as chicken, beef, veggie, cheese, and more. We also offer salads, soups, drinks, and desserts to complete your meal.

Brandy's Crunchwrap Supremes is a family-owned and operated restaurant that started in 2023. We are passionate about serving delicious and satisfying food to our customers. We use only the finest ingredients and prepare everything fresh daily. Our goal is to make you happy and full with our crunchwraps.
